Welcome to the World of Kitchenware and Houseware!

About Us

Welcome to KK Enterprises, your trusted partner in kitchenware and houseware solutions since 1990. We take immense pride in being the leading Super Stockists and Distributors of renowned brands in India. Our journey, spanning over three decades, has been marked by a commitment to excellence, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Our History

Established in 1990, KK Enterprises embarked on a mission to bring the finest kitchenware and houseware products to homes across India. Over the years, we have nurtured strong partnerships with some of the most trusted and esteemed brands in the industry. These partnerships have allowed us to offer an extensive and diverse range of products that cater to the ever-evolving needs and preferences of our customers.

Our Commitment

At KK Enterprises, our commitment extends beyond just being a distributor. We are dedicated to delivering unparalleled service, ensuring that our customers receive the highest quality products at competitive prices. We continuously strive to stay at the forefront of the industry, offering the latest innovations and trends in kitchenware and houseware.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the preferred destination for every household's kitchenware and houseware needs. We aim to continue growing our portfolio of brands and products while maintaining the trust and satisfaction of our valued customers.
